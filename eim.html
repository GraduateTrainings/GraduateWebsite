    • Cambridge English Programme Outline

      A strong command of the English language is a crucial form of communication today, locally and internationally. The ability to comprehend and speak the language fluently and effectively instills confidence in learners as communication skills in the English language progressively improves with the Cambridge English Programme for Adults from beginners, pre-intermediate, intermediate and upper- intermediate levels.

      With emphasis on listening and speaking but with many opportunities for reading and writing, the Cambridge English programme addresses the learners’ everyday language needs. The language is systematically introduced to the participant through a variety of topics.

      The oral component of the topics focuses on our current life-style and environment. The written component revolves around the topic with emphasis on comprehension topped with grammar practice exercises.

      The programme also provides a wealth of authentic settings, meaningful communication activities and ample opportunity to personalize learning materials so learners will be equipped with the language they will need to actively use daily.

      Objectives

      • To develop effective communication skills in the English Language.
      • To speak confidently and possess the ability to express oneself in English.
      • To write with precision and accuracy relevant in informal/ formal communication.

      Methodology

      The Cambridge Language Programme is conducted via:
      • INTERGRATED/PARTICIPATIVE ACTIVITIES in everyday situations include reading, listening and writing tasks, as well as discussion, problem-solving and role-play.
      • ROLE PLAY is an essential part of the course. Most tasks in the integrated activities are done in pairs or small groups, so that everyone has a chance to participate.
      • FORMAL INSTRUCTION introduces vocabulary via the texts and exercises.
      • FUNCTIONS are introduced relevant to the practice of functional language required in various situations via individual, pair-work and/or small group presentation.
      • LISTENING SKILLS are further developed through carefully selected exercises recorded on CDs to compliment the programmes.
      • REVIEWS through ‘Check your progress’ and ‘Unit Checks’ are conducted regularly to reinforce knowledge and acquisition of skills.
      • PLACEMENT TESTS conducted prior to commencement of classes ensures that learners are placed into the appropriate English Programme according to participant’s level of proficiency.
      • ORGANISED LEARNING whereby each level contains 16 units grouped into four modules. Each module is allocated 20 hours to complete. Each level requires 80hours to complete.

      Duration

      Cambridge English In Mind: 40 Hours per level (Starter/Level 1,2,3,4)

      Option 1: Face-to-face (F2F) classroom instruction: 5 full days per level

      Option 2: 10 Half-day sessions
